Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 07/03/2025

PSN ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application ("App").

1. Information Collection and Use

Our App does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All user-generated data, are stored locally on your device using an SQLite database. We do not transmit, process, or share this data with any third parties.

2. Internet Connection

Our App operates mostly offline. However, an internet connection is required to log in and download data. No user data is uploaded or transmitted over the internet.

3. No Advertisements

We do not display advertisements in the App. There are no third-party trackers, analytics tools, or any mechanisms that collect user information.

4. Permissions

Our App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera Access: The App may request access to your device's camera for specific features that require capturing images or scanning QR codes.
  • Storage Access: This permission is required solely for storing user preferences, within your device. We do not access any other personal data or files.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security measures. We recommend using standard security practices such as device encryption and password protection.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, and the latest version will always be available on our GitHub Pages. Your continued use of the App after any modifications signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
